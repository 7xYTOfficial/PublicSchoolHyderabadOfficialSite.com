Imran Larik

Current Principal Of Public School Hyderabad.

Sir Imran Larik, He has served this school as principle for 3 years now and is still the ongoing Principle for PSH. He has done renovations to the school as IBA Sukkur Universcity took over the school. He is doing an amazing job as principle, taking care of students edutcation and mental health as well as teachers pay and well being. He has made education much better in these years, Moreover he has bought hostels for border students and fixed roads for day scholars.
